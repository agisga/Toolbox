## Annotate snp into gene regions
## $1: snp file with at least 3 columns(include header): CHR  BP SNP, where BP is base position. More columns can be included following the required 3 columns.
## $2: gene annotation file with 4 columns(include header): Chr  begin_pos end_pos gene_name
## Chr is purely number, do not include the "Chr" as prefix.
## plink2 --annotate reads a variant-based PLINK report, and writes an annotated version to plink.annot.

plink2 --annotate $1 ranges=$2
